Erotic Grief Ritual
Brique par Brique

Title: Erotic Grief Ritual

Organizer: Jess Lieu

Description: Within this communal portal we’ll be using Kai Cheng’s Tree of Cultural Erotic Growth as a tool for curiosity and dreaming. As we reflect on our erotic selves and the beliefs we are ready to let go of, we’ll use communal ritual to release with gratitude and the power of our collective intention. There will be journalling/doodling, sharing, movement and voice. The intention of the ritual is to honour our personal grief, while co creating a container to witness one another in our losses. The invitation is to follow your pleasure and feel as good as possible in the holding. May we compost what we no longer need to make space for our deepest desires Please note: all of you is welcome and choosing your level of participation is encouraged. There is no right or wrong way to feel grief, no hierarchy in experiences, no fixing or advising. Jess Lieu (she/her), a Somatic Pleasure Educator and residing in Tiohtià:ke/Mooniyang/Montreal. With a dedication to fostering bodily wisdom and joy, Jess illuminates the pathways of pleasure, desire, and consent through intimate, one-on-one explorations, bodywork and workshops. Informed by Somatic Sex Education, Energetic Ecology, BodyMind Centering and death studies, she entrusts the body as a portal to personal and collective transformation. Price : Sliding scale $1-$25 See more here.
